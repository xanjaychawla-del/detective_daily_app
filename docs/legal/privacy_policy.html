Detective Daily — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Detective Daily ("the app") is a detective mystery game published by xanjay.chawla. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and how you can control or delete it.

Summary

You can play Detective Daily entirely anonymously — the app creates an anonymous device session automatically and no personal information is required to solve cases. Registering with an email address is optional and only unlocks saving your progress/tier across devices. The app does not use location, camera, microphone, or contacts, and does not show ads.

How case content is generated

Some cases are authored by us; others are generated on request using Google's Gemini AI model, and case narration (spoken dialogue) is synthesized using Google Cloud Text-to-Speech. These requests process case story text only — no personal information about you is sent to these services as part of generating or narrating a case.

Where your data is stored

App data is stored using Supabase (backend infrastructure) and Google Firebase (analytics and crash reporting), both of which act as data processors on our behalf under industry-standard security practices, including encryption in transit.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Data is only shared with the infrastructure providers listed above, solely to operate the app.

Your choices

  • Play as a guest — no account or email required. Your progress is tied to your device's anonymous session.
  • Register — link an email address (or Google/Apple/Facebook sign-in, where available) to save your progress and access it if you reinstall or switch devices.
  • Delete your account — from the Register screen, once signed in, tap "Delete account" to permanently and immediately delete your account, play history, ratings, and tier data. This cannot be undone.

Children's privacy

Detective Daily is a general-audience mystery/puzzle game not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app changes. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
